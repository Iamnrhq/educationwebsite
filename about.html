SAHARA INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Focused on Excellence and Learning

Located in Banglore, amidst the backdrop of pristine surroundings and away from the bustling locales, Cochin Public School is one among the top cbse schools in Kochi today. The foundation of the school goes back to 1992 by A.M Aboobacker who wanted to create a learning environment that conveys academics beyond books. From academic foundation to development of your child’s intellectual and emotional personality, the school is all about an exceptional learning environ in true sense.

The expansive and modern campus of Cochin Public School includes some of the finest facilities. These facilities are build to channelize your child’s holistic and social development to the fullest.

The school has 68 classrooms, organized library, integrated cafeterias, 5 laboratories, outdoor playgrounds, halls, 10 av room etc. In Tandem to the facilities, the school also makes use of various curricular and extracurricular activities. We have educators who are well trained to take care of this. Broadly these activities include programs of music, art, athletics, information technology and various service schemes. This experience lets your child to explore their existing skills as well as discover new ones.

To make the world a better place is what we aim to develop in our students at Cochin Public School. Till today (when we are writing this copy) 20 batches of class X and 19 batches of class XII have passed out in flying colors. With our agenda to provide long term solutions to academic challenges, we sail every day to contribute in bringing out many such colors.

Our Core Values:

The following core values are the foundation of the educational motives of Cochin Public School

  • Provide a safe, creative, and unique learning environment that consists of numerous curricular and extra-curricular opportunities

  • Promote a culture that fosters students to develop both intellectually and socially.

  • Offer a collaborative community relationship between students, parents, faculty and administrators to enhance the educational process.

  • Utilize latest teaching styles and facilities to let your child experience the fullest.

We strictly adhere to:

  • Provide each student with knowledge and quality education to become capable.

  • Support, Respect and celebrate each other’s achievements.

  • Act with integrity and understand each learner’s innate potentiality.

  • Maintain positive, compassionate and caring relationship towards students as well as parents.

  • Give students opportunities to explore and develop their skills.

Our Vision

To provide education for all with emphasis on sports and athletics along with academics keeping in mind that they play an important role in the all-round development of a child. To prepare children to meet the global challenges ahead of them in today's changing scenario both in India and the World.

Our Mission

The SAHARA International School provides education for all children through innovative and creative programs that will empower them to make correct choices and become assets to society and the country at large.

Our Goal

To give opportunities to all the students to explore and build on their abilities and strength. To motivate, empower and develop the life skills to realize their potentials and to excel anywhere in the world.
